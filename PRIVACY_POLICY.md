# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 19, 2026

## Introduction
Human Typer ("we", "our", or "the App") is a Windows desktop application designed to simulate human typing behavior. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

## Data Collection
**Human Typer does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

-   **No Personal Information:** We do not collect names, email addresses, or any other personally identifiable information.
-   **No Usage Data:** We do not track how you use the application.
-   **No Network Activity:** The application runs entirely locally on your device and does not connect to the internet for any data reporting or analytics.

## Local Processing
All text processing and typing simulation occur locally on your machine. The text you input into the application remains in your computer's memory and is not sent to any external servers.
